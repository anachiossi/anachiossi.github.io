Production sound mixer and boom operator based in Rome, Italy. Born in Brazil, I've spent 14 years working across feature films, series, documentaries, and advertising — in Portuguese, English, Spanish, Italian, et un petit peu du Français.

Before moving into sound, I worked as a camera assistant — as a focus puller, video assist, and loader. That background gives me a particular sensitivity to the relationship between image and audio on set, and helps me communicate naturally with directors and cinematographers from day one.

Over 65 projects, I've collaborated with 119 production companies. My work has reached audiences on Netflix, Disney+, HBO, and Amazon Prime.
